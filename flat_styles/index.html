Notes & known issues

  1. Numbers in the Typography: Lists ol have questionable horizontal alignment.
  2. Buttons in sets have an uneven separating gap which seems to be affected by font weight and/or size or page zoom level. This looks like a pixel calculation which results in either 1px or 2px depending on the text/zoom conditions.
  3. Buttons in sets don't work so gracefully with button--outline and could do with additional work on the borders/separating gaps.
